## Caveats

- OEWS uses **SOC 2010** through May 2019 and **SOC 2018** from May 2020 onward — see `soc_vintage` column.
- Wage and employment values can be **suppressed** (rendered as `*` / `**` upstream); they appear as `NaN` here.
- A subset of newer files include `OWN_CODE`; we prefer ownership `1235` (all ownerships) where present.
- For matching to AIOE (SOC 2010), apply the BLS SOC 2010 → 2018 crosswalk before joining post-2019 rows.
