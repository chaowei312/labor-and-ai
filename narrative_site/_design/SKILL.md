Two non-negotiable rules from the editorial brief:
1. **Vocabulary discipline.** Use "capital concentration / labor share / substitution vs augmentation / exposure / positioning". Never use "AI throne / cyberpunk / ultra-rich / robots". The hook can speak to the worry; the chart language must remain professional.
2. **Data honesty.** Every wage chart names "nominal" or "real". Every wage-percentile chart names OEWS top-coding (≈ $208 k in 2018). Every claim about post-2023 dynamics names that the OEWS panel ends in 2023. The reader leaves with a watchlist, not a verdict.
